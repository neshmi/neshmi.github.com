about me

Perhaps I'm confused. Perhaps not. I'm an anthropological archaeologist and a Rubyist. It is hard to say which one I love more. Right now, I combine these two passions in my life, using my love of programming, technology and electronics to aid in archaeological fieldwork. This can make for a more objective science in the field and aid in open data sharing back home. I authored [OpenDig](http://www.opendig.org) to help with the field collection which now has taken form in the way of three different applications, a full Ruby on Rails application, a CouchApp, and an iOS application. As more tools are created they will be posted to this space. Stay tuned!!
